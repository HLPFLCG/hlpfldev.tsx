Our Story

HLPFL was founded in 2018 with a simple but powerful mission: make professional web development accessible to first-time founders. We saw too many talented entrepreneurs struggle with expensive, complicated website solutions that didn't meet their needs or budgets.

Our founder, just 18 at the time, had experienced firsthand the challenges of starting a business with limited resources. After successfully launching several ventures, they discovered a passion for helping other entrepreneurs navigate the digital landscape.

Based in Grand Rapids, Michigan, we're 100% US-based and proud of it. No outsourcing, no language barriers, no timezone issues. Just quality work from people who understand your journey because we've been there too.

Today, at 24 years old with 6+ years of entrepreneurial experience, our founder leads a team that's delivered over 100 successful projects, maintaining a 98% client satisfaction rate while keeping prices affordable for first-time founders.
